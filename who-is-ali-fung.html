About Ali

Hi! I’m sometimes Alexandra, mostly Ali, and occasionally Alex if you ask someone who doesn’t know me very well. I design things, but most importantly, I ask questions. I’m a people-person, and that’s the biggest reason why I love what I do. Whether it’s a user, a stakeholder, or a fellow team member, I incorporate their needs, their perspectives, and their values in everything I do.
‍
In my free time, you can find me wandering the streets of New York City in search of pop-up galleries, cultivating my vast collection of indoor plants, and planning my next meal.

My values

Focus on the people

The first most important person in any project is the user. A close second place is my teammates. Close collaboration and a user-centered focus are the secret ingredients to any successful product.

Deliver value beyond wires

I believe my role as a designer reaches far beyond screens. I see my role as to advocate for the user, test, problem-solve, and encourage transparency and user centricity through everything I do.

Test and learn

Even when unexpected problems pop up (they always do) you’ll find me striving for a double diamond approach of learning, iterating, and delivering value.

My not-so-secret sauce

I’m a strong believer in an iterative process that involves frequent stakeholder visibility, data-driven decision-making, frequent co-creation, and constant user feedback.
